U-LIME 📱/💻

by: El Mehdi El Garda , Reda Mokere

Tropical soils are facing a serious problem which is soil acidity. The application of limestone can be a solution to increase the soil pH. Limestone is a carbonate sedimentary rock that is the primary source of lime. After applying an adequate quantity of this substance, it will be dissolved and neutralizes the acidity by releasing a base into the soil.

This application has two interfaces, a website and a mobile application
